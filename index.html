The difference between paddings, margins, and borders:

A content is bounded by padding which is again bounded by the border. The border is again bounded by the margin.
Border is the strip that goes around your div/element of the content. Padding is the space between the content and the border. Margin is the space outside the border of an element. If you need to add some space around an image or a text area, you can add a margin to the relevant item.
